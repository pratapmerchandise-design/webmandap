The Process

One clear path from first conversation to a fully live presence.

A website project should not leave you coordinating designers, developers, hosting providers, domain sellers, or Google setup on your own. We take ownership of the moving parts from the moment we understand your business to the moment your website is live, searchable, and ready to represent you properly.

Below is walkthrough of how a Web Mandap project moves from conversation to launch.

Strategy → Design → Build → Launch Live website in as little as 5 days!
01 Business immersion
Before we design anything

We understand the business behind the website.

Before any design begins, we take time to understand your business, your audience, your goals, and what sets you apart. A great website starts with clarity—not assumptions.

  • Your business model
  • Audience & decision makers
  • Brand perception
  • Website objectives
02 Feature workshop
Planning it with the people who know it best

We discuss every feature with the owner or handling team.

Together with you, we map out every important feature, page, enquiry flow, integration, and customer journey before development begins. Every decision is made with purpose.

  • Pages & navigation
  • Forms & lead flows
  • WhatsApp / calls / booking
  • Visual direction
03 First draft
Turning the plan into something you can see

We create the first version around your brand—not a template.

We transform the strategy into a working first draft tailored to your brand. It gives you a clear picture of the design, structure, and overall experience before refinement.

  • Custom page structure
  • Visual design direction
  • Responsive layout planning
  • Initial content placement
04 Refine & build
Feedback becomes polish

We refine the details, then build the final website properly.

Your feedback shapes the final product. We refine the design, polish every interaction, and complete a fully custom website that performs beautifully across every device.

  • Feedback-led refinement
  • Custom coded build
  • Mobile optimisation
  • Final quality checks
05 Launch & visibility
The part most people leave you to figure out

We take care of domain, hosting, launch, and being found.

Once approved, we handle everything needed to take your website live—from domain and hosting to SSL, deployment, and Google indexing—so your business is ready from day one.

  • Domain purchase & connection
  • Hosting & SSL setup
  • Google Search Console
  • Sitemap & indexing basics
One team. Every moving part handled with intention.

From the first call to the moment someone finds your website online, Web Mandap stays responsible for the experience—not just the visuals.
